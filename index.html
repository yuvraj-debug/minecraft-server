Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our server

How do I join the server?

Simply open Minecraft, go to Multiplayer, click "Add Server", then enter our IP: play.example.net. Click "Done" and then "Join Server".

What version of Minecraft do you support?

We currently support Minecraft Java Edition 1.19.2 and above. We update to new versions as soon as our plugins are compatible.

Are there any rules I should know about?

Yes! We have a comprehensive set of rules that promote fair play and a positive community. You can view them on our Discord server.

How can I become a staff member?

We regularly recruit staff from our active player base. Keep an eye on our Discord announcements for when applications open.
